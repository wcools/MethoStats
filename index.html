Consultancy

Get an expert in setting up behavioral and social science research, whether experimental, observational or naturalistic, to ensure that you get the information you need while dealing with constraints you are facing.

Data Analysis

Get an expert on a wide variety of techniques and theories to describe data statically, to interpret the results scientifically, to do the statistical reasoning, visualisations, and to provide fully documented and reproducible code in R.

Tutoring

Get an expert in communicating research and statistics to varied audiences ranging from applied statisticians in companies, to students at university and even policy makers. For DIY researchers, get an expert to provide workshops or on site training.

Automatisation

Get an expert in the automatisation of data analyses, data management and reporting. Automatisation avoids repetitive work, errors and time loss, and it promotes uniformity.

Experience

Projects : Data Analyses and Consultancy

I was awarded several smaller projects to assist in ongoing research and to take responsibility for the quantitative part of their studies. The most significant client was Selor who is constantly improving the quality of the testing procedures.

Research in Methodology @ CMES, KU Leuven

Bootstrap experiments to compare various types of bootstrap for the estimation of single case data using multilevel data analyses. This research was done at the Centre for Methodology of Educational Research (3 months, 2015).

Research in Psychometrics @ CEO, KU Leuven

Data analyses and consultancy related to the study of learning behavior in various schools. This research was done at the Centre for Educational Effectiveness and Evaluation (4 months, 2013-14).

Research in Psychometrics @ iTec, KU Leuven

Development of an interactive language learning tool based on the analyses of longitudinally observed test scores. This research was done at the Centre for Interactive Technologies (24 months, 2011-13).

Research in Quantitative Psychology @ OKP, KU Leuven

Formal modeling of the evolution of emotions over time and its interindividual variability using a dynamical systems approach on longitudinally observed emotion state data. This research was done at the Research Group Quantitative Psychology and Individual Differences (14 months, 2009-10).

Research in Methodology of Educational Research @ CMES, KU Leuven

Simulation studies to compare efficiency of multilevel designs in estimating fixed and random parameters for various choices of group sizes at the various levels. This research was done at the Centre for Methodology of Educational Research (4 academic years, 2004-08).

Research in Educational Sociology @ CESO, KU Leuven.

Survey research on the experienced effect (by teaching staff) of high-school mergers in Flanders (Belgium). This research was done at the Centre for Sociology of Education (15 months, 2000-01).
