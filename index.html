         TOS (pdf)

Consultancy

Get an expert in setting up behavioral and social science research, whether experimental, observational or naturalistic, to ensure that you get the information you need while dealing with constraints you are facing.

Statistically: What is the best way to analyse the data ? How should the results be visualized ? Methodology: What is the best way to collect your data ? Does it guarantee that you get the answers you are looking for ?

Data Analysis

Get an expert on a wide variety of techniques and theories to describe data statically, to interpret the results scientifically, to do the statistical reasoning, visualisations, and to provide fully documented and reproducible code in R.

How to perform the analyses ? Can the data be used as such, or does it require further data management ? How to best deal with the results ?

Tutoring

Get an expert in communicating research and statistics to varied audiences ranging from applied statisticians in companies, to students at university and even policy makers. For DIY researchers, get an expert to provide workshops or on site training.

Would you care for a Workshop, lectures or presentations to address your topic of interest ? Get the theory, get the code !

Automatization

Get an expert in the automatization of data analyses, data management and reporting. Automatization avoids repetitive work, errors and time loss, and it promotes uniformity.

Can it be automated, and how ? Automated data collection, reporting, or even data analyses sometimes are possible.

Consultancy and Data Analysis

I was awarded several projects to assist in ongoing research and to take responsibility for the quantitative part of these studies.

Independent

As a freelancer I have contributed in various types of research, both providing advice and running the analyses. This for example included the analysis of observed workload in nursing, the programming of automated test item analysis, running bootstrap experiments, and more.

External

As a statistical consultant at university I have dealt with various types of research, including medical, psychological, and industrial research. This for example included the analysis of pain measurements over time, reviewing experiments on effects of food constituents, comparing the appreciation of different products, analyzing changes in mathematical test results.

Internal

As a researcher I have helped fellow researchers in educational research and linguists with their experimental and observational studies. This for example required me to run some analyses, write programs to automatically process data from online data registration tools, give advice on experimental design.

Research

I was a researcher in various research centers at university, dealing with different types of data (experimental, observational, naturalistic - cross-sectional, longitudinal) and many different types of models. References to publications in international journals and talks at international congresses are included in the resume for download.

Methodology

With simulation and bootstrap experiments I have focused on sample size issues for multilevel and single case design within a meta-analysis approach.

Psychometrics

Using IRT-family models I have studied how to evaluate the learning of second language and mathematics test data. The aim was to automate the process in order to develop an adaptive learning environment.

Quantitative Psychology

The evolution of emotions over time and its interindividual variability I have considered using a dynamical systems approach on longitudinally observed emotion state data.

Experimental Psychology

Experiments were used to study social phenomenon like discrimination and perceptual phenomenon like the importance of novelty.

Sociology

The experienced effect of mergers in high-schools I studied quantitatively using survey research.
